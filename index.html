Facility/Cost

Technicolabs Softwares offers top-notch web development services to enhance your online presence. Our skilled team creates visually appealing, user-friendly websites with the latest technologies. From custom designs to e-commerce solutions, we ensure your digital success.

Web Development

The cost of developing a website varies depending on the complexity and features. A basic informational site typically costs between $100 to $200, while a standard business site ranges from $200 to $500. E-commerce sites with features like shopping carts fall between $350 to $1,000, and custom web applications can cost from $700 to over $1,700. Premium enterprise-level websites often exceed $1,700. These prices vary based on project complexity, added features, and services like SEO and maintenance.

UI/UX

The cost of UI/UX design varies based on complexity and expertise. Basic design for a small app or website typically costs between $100 to $250. More comprehensive projects, like a full website or complex app, range from $300 to $700. High-end, custom UI/UX design for large-scale projects or enterprise applications can exceed $700, going up to $1,500 or more. These costs often include user research, wireframing, prototyping, and final design implementation.

Mobile App Dev

The cost of mobile app development varies based on the app's complexity and features. Simple apps with basic functionality typically cost between $150 to $500. Medium complexity apps, with features like user authentication and API integration, range from $500 to $1,500. High-end, complex apps, including advanced features such as real-time updates, payment gateways, and custom animations, can cost between $1,400 to $3,500 or more. These estimates cover the full development process, from design to deployment and testing.

Digital Marketing

The cost of digital marketing services varies based on scope and strategy. Basic services, such as social media management and basic SEO, typically range from $100 to $200 per month. More comprehensive packages, including content creation, advanced SEO, and PPC campaigns, cost between $200 to $500 per month. High-end strategies for large businesses, involving extensive campaigns and detailed analytics, can exceed $500, reaching up to $1,500 or more per month. These costs include the planning, execution, and monitoring of digital marketing activities.
